Membership Agreement and Church Policies

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Ngaru Anglican Church. These Terms and Conditions govern your membership and use of our church services, both physical and digital. By becoming a member, you agree to abide by these terms.

Important: These terms are legally binding. Please read them carefully before agreeing. If you have any questions, contact our church administration.

Membership Terms

1. Eligibility Requirements

  • Profession of faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior
  • Baptism in any recognized Christian denomination
  • Completion of new members' orientation class
  • Regular attendance for at least 3 months
  • Commitment to Anglican doctrine and practices
  • Agreement to support the church through prayer and giving

2. Membership Rights

  • Full participation in Holy Communion
  • Voting rights in congregational meetings
  • Eligibility for leadership positions
  • Access to pastoral care and counseling
  • Participation in church sacraments (baptism, confirmation, marriage)
  • Access to church facilities for approved events

3. Membership Responsibilities

  • Regular Sunday worship attendance
  • Financial support through tithes and offerings
  • Participation in at least one ministry or service group
  • Maintenance of Christian conduct and witness
  • Respect for church leadership and fellow members
  • Regular prayer for the church and its ministries

Code of Conduct

1. Christian Behavior

All members are expected to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with Christian values, showing love, respect, and integrity in all interactions.

2. Church Property & Facilities

  • Treat all church property with respect and care
  • Follow facility use guidelines and booking procedures
  • Report any damages or maintenance needs immediately
  • Clean up after using any church facilities
  • No smoking, alcohol, or illegal substances on premises

3. Communication & Conflict Resolution

  • Respectful communication at all times
  • Follow Matthew 18:15-17 for conflict resolution
  • No gossip, slander, or divisive speech
  • Bring concerns to appropriate church leadership
  • Maintain confidentiality when required

Digital Services & Online Conduct

1. Member Portal Usage

  • Keep login credentials secure and confidential
  • No sharing of accounts with others
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Use only for church-related activities
  • Respect others' privacy in the directory

2. Data Protection

  • We use Firebase for secure data storage
  • Your personal information is encrypted
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • You control what information is visible to others
  • Regular security audits are conducted

3. Media & Photography

  • By attending events, you consent to photography
  • Photos may be used for church publications and website
  • Opt-out available for those who prefer not to be photographed
  • No unauthorized recording of services
  • Respect others' privacy when sharing photos online

Financial Terms

1. Giving & Contributions

  • Giving is voluntary but encouraged as spiritual discipline
  • All contributions are tax-deductible (receipts provided)
  • Online giving through secure payment gateways
  • No pressure or obligation to give specific amounts
  • Financial transparency through annual reports

2. Financial Accountability

  • All funds managed by elected finance committee
  • Regular financial audits by independent auditors
  • Budget approval by congregational vote
  • Transparent reporting of all income and expenses
  • Strict adherence to financial policies and procedures

Discipline & Termination

1. Grounds for Discipline

  • Persistent unrepentant sin
  • False teaching or heresy
  • Division or strife within the congregation
  • Criminal activity
  • Abuse of church position or resources
  • Violation of these terms and conditions

2. Disciplinary Process

  • Private counsel and warning (Matthew 18:15-17)
  • Involvement of church leadership if unresolved
  • Temporary suspension of membership privileges
  • Restoration process for repentant members
  • Formal removal only after all steps exhausted

3. Voluntary Withdrawal

  • Members may withdraw membership at any time
  • Written notice requested but not required
  • Exit interview available upon request
  • Transfer letter provided for other churches
  • No refund of contributions upon withdrawal

Legal Terms

1. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of Kenya. Any disputes shall be resolved through Christian mediation before legal action.

2. Disclaimer of Liability

  • Church not liable for personal injury on premises (except negligence)
  • Not responsible for lost or stolen personal property
  • Online services provided "as is" without warranties
  • No guarantee of specific spiritual outcomes
  • Members participate in activities at their own risk

3. Amendments

  • Terms may be updated annually
  • Members notified of significant changes
  • Continued membership implies acceptance of updates
  • Major changes subject to congregational vote
  • Previous versions archived for reference
