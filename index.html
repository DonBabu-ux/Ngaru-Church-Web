Welcome to Ngaru Anglican Church

Rooted in Christ, Serving in Love.

Who We Are

Ngaru Anglican Church is a Christ-centered congregation committed to biblical teaching, faithful worship, and compassionate service.

We are a community of faith, hope, and love, standing firm in faith and growing together in God’s grace.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in faith, and serve our community through love and good works.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant Anglican church where lives are transformed by Christ and generations are built in faith.

Our Core Values

Faithfulness to Scripture and Anglican doctrine

Reverent worship and prayer

Love, unity, and service to all

Spiritual growth for every generation
