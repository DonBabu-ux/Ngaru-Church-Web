Who We Are

Ngaru Anglican Church is a Christ-centered congregation deeply committed to biblical teaching, reverent worship, and compassionate service. As part of the Anglican Church of Kenya, we uphold the rich traditions of the Anglican faith while engaging dynamically with our contemporary context.

We are a diverse community of believers united by our faith in Jesus Christ, standing firm in God's truth, and growing together in His grace and love.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ faithfully, nurture believers in spiritual maturity, and serve our community through Christ-like love and compassionate action.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, growing Anglican community where lives are transformed by Christ's love, disciples are multiplied, and generations are built up in faith, hope, and love.

Our Core Values

Faithfulness to Scripture and Anglican doctrine

Reverent worship and prayer

Love, unity, and service to all

Spiritual growth for every generation
