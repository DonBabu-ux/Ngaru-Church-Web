Privacy Policy

Your Data Protection and Privacy Rights

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

At Ngaru Anglican Church, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in accordance with Kenyan data protection laws and Christian ethical standards.

Note: By using our services and becoming a member, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly:

Identity Information

  • Full name and title
  • Contact details (email, phone)
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Home address
  • Photograph (optional)

Spiritual Information

  • Baptism and confirmation status
  • Previous church affiliation
  • Ministry interests
  • Testimony and spiritual journey
  • Prayer requests

Family Information

  • Marital status
  • Spouse and children details
  • Emergency contacts
  • Family member relationships

2. Automatic Collection

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and services
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Location Data: General location for service optimization
  • Cookies: For functionality and analytics (see Cookie Policy)

3. Financial Information

  • Giving history and patterns
  • Payment method (not stored by us)
  • Tax receipt information
  • Pledge commitments (if any)

How We Use Your Information

Pastoral Care

  • Provide spiritual guidance
  • Visit sick and homebound
  • Crisis intervention
  • Prayer support

Communication

  • Church announcements
  • Event invitations
  • Ministry updates
  • Prayer chain alerts

Administration

  • Membership records
  • Service planning
  • Ministry coordination
  • Resource allocation

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent
  • Contract: Necessary for membership agreement
  • Legitimate Interest: Church administration and pastoral care
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with Kenyan laws

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not and will never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Internal Sharing

  • Church Leadership: Pastors and elders for pastoral care
  • Ministry Leaders: For ministry coordination
  • Administration Team: For church operations
  • Directory: Limited info with other members (with consent)

2. External Service Providers

  • Firebase: Secure data storage and authentication
  • Email Service: For church communications
  • Payment Processors: For secure online giving
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymous data)

3. Legal Requirements

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government authorities (when required by law)
  • Protection of rights and safety
  • Investigation of potential violations

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to sensitive data
  • Firebase Security: Industry-standard security practices
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability testing
  • Staff Training: Data protection training for all staff

Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Log out of shared devices
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Update your information regularly

Your Data Protection Rights

Right to Access

Request copies of your personal data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data

Right to Restrict

Limit how we use your data

Right to Portability

Receive your data in usable format

Right to Object

Object to certain processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Contact our Data Protection Officer
  • Use the privacy settings in your member portal
  • Submit a written request to the church office
  • Allow 30 days for processing requests

Data Retention

Retention Periods

  • Active Members: Data retained while active
  • Inactive Members: 7 years after last activity
  • Financial Records: 7 years for tax purposes
  • Sacramental Records: Permanent (baptism, marriage)
  • Prayer Requests: 1 year unless renewed

Data Deletion

  • Secure deletion when retention period ends
  • Anonymization for statistical purposes
  • Archival for historical church records
  • Notification before significant deletion

Children's Privacy

Special Protection

We take extra precautions with children's data:

  • Parental consent required for children under 18
  • Limited data collection for children
  • Separate Sunday School database with restricted access
  • No photos of children online without explicit consent
  • Special training for children's ministry workers

International Data Transfers

Firebase Services

Firebase may process data outside Kenya. We ensure:

  • Firebase complies with international standards
  • Data remains encrypted during transfer
  • Adequate safeguards are in place
  • You are informed of any significant changes

Changes to This Policy

  • Annual review and update of this policy
  • Notification of significant changes via email
  • Updated date displayed at top of policy
  • Continued use implies acceptance of updates
  • Archive of previous versions available on request

Contact & Complaints

Complaints

If unsatisfied with our response, you may contact:

  • Office of the Data Protection Commissioner Kenya
  • Anglican Church of Kenya Headquarters
  • Local Government Authorities

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your experience.

Types We Use

  • Essential: Required for site functionality
  • Preference: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Marketing: None - we don't use marketing cookies

Cookie Control

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.
