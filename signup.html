Membership Benefits

  • Full participation in church sacraments
  • Voting rights in church meetings
  • Eligibility for leadership positions
  • Pastoral care and support
  • Regular church communications
  • Access to member-only events

What to Expect

  • Your application will be reviewed by our membership committee
  • You'll be contacted for a brief welcome interview
  • New member orientation sessions are held monthly
  • You'll be assigned a church family mentor
  • Formal welcome during a Sunday service

Membership Requirements

  • Profession of faith in Jesus Christ
  • Baptism in any Christian denomination
  • Regular attendance for at least 3 months
  • Completion of new members' class
  • Commitment to church's vision and values

Next Steps After Submission

After submitting this form, our membership secretary will contact you within one week to schedule a welcome interview. New members are formally received during our monthly communion services.
